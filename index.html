OUR STORY

Born from Necessity, Crafted with Love

Confronted with persistent skin issues, the Lichtenauer family decided to take matters into their own hands. We began crafting our own skincare products using only the finest natural ingredients, sourced with the highest standards.

The results exceeded our expectations. Today, we are proud to share our experience with you, continuously improving our offerings through your valuable feedback.

Our Philosophy

100% Natural

No artificial or synthetic components. Only pure, carefully selected botanical ingredients.

Minimal Processing

We believe in preserving the integrity of our ingredients through the least necessary transformation.

Artisan Crafted

Every product is handmade in small batches at our home in Monts Sutton with personal attention to detail.

OUR INGREDIENTS

Nature's Finest, Selected with Care

Every ingredient in our products is carefully chosen for its beneficial properties. We maintain strict standards for quality, sustainability, and efficacy.

Botanical Oils

Cold-pressed rosehip, apricot kernel, and lavender oils rich in vitamins and antioxidants.

Natural Waxes

Pure cera alba (beeswax) and plant-based alternatives for texture and protection.

Butters & Extracts

Nutrient-rich butyrospermum parkii (shea butter) and concentrated herbal extracts.
